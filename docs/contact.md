## Project Inquiries

Interested in working together on a project? Here's what I typically need to know:

### For Development Projects

1. **Project Overview**: What are you building?
2. **Platform**: iOS, Android, or both?
3. **Timeline**: When do you need it completed?
4. **Budget**: What's your budget range?
5. **Existing Codebase**: Starting from scratch or existing project?

### For Consulting

1. **Challenge**: What problem are you trying to solve?
2. **Current Setup**: What's your current technical stack?
3. **Goals**: What outcomes are you looking for?
4. **Timeline**: How urgent is the need?
