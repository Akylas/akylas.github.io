# Oenoneo

![Oenoneo Logo](/img/portfolio/oenoneo/logo.png)

## Overview

**Oenoneo** is a sophisticated wine management and cellar tracking application designed for wine enthusiasts and collectors. The app helps users catalog their wine collection, track tasting notes, and manage their cellar inventory.

### Project Details

- **Platform**: iOS & Android
- **Technology**: Titanium Mobile
- **Release Date**: January 2017
- **Category**: Lifestyle & Food

## Features

### Wine Management

- 📦 **Cellar Inventory**: Track your wine collection with detailed information
- 🍷 **Wine Database**: Comprehensive wine information and details
- 📝 **Tasting Notes**: Record and organize your wine tasting experiences
- 🏷️ **Labels & Photos**: Store wine labels and bottle photos
- 📊 **Collection Analytics**: View statistics about your collection
- 🔍 **Search & Filter**: Easily find wines in your collection

### Smart Features

- Barcode scanning for quick wine entry
- Purchase tracking and valuation
- Drinking window recommendations
- Pairing suggestions
- Location tracking for wine storage

## Technical Implementation

**Framework**: Titanium Mobile  
**Backend**: Cloud synchronization  
**Storage**: Local SQLite database  
**Camera**: Native camera integration for labels  

### Technical Highlights

- Cross-platform native performance
- Offline-first architecture
- Real-time synchronization
- Image processing for label recognition
- Secure data storage

- Wine collectors and enthusiasts
- Sommeliers and wine professionals
- Restaurant wine managers

## Impact

Oenoneo helps wine lovers organize and appreciate their collections, making wine management simple and enjoyable. The app has been used to catalog thousands of bottles worldwide.

### Key Benefits

- **Organization**: Keep your wine collection organized and accessible
- **Knowledge**: Learn about your wines with detailed information
- **Planning**: Know when to drink wines at their peak
- **Sharing**: Share your collection and notes with friends

## User Experience

The app features an elegant, intuitive interface designed specifically for wine enthusiasts:

- Visual wine catalog with label photos
- Easy-to-use tasting note editor
- Quick access to frequently accessed wines
- Beautiful data visualizations
